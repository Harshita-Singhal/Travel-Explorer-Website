FAQ

How do I save my trip plans?

Use the Plan Trip page to enter your destination, date, and activities, then click "Save Trip Plan". Your plans will appear under My Trips.

Is my data saved permanently?

Currently, plans are saved in your browser's local storage, so they persist between visits on the same device and browser.

Can I upload my own photos?

This demo does not support uploading photos, but it's a great feature to add in the future!
